38. Clean Dirty Pots (Anti-Grease)

Image: A jar of homemade cleaning paste (like 'The Pink Stuff')

Ingredients
  • 1 Tbsp. Cornstarch (Maizena)
  • 1 cup Dish Soap
  • 1 cup Baking Soda
  • Aromatizing Extracts
Method
  1. Mix all ingredients to form a paste.
  2. Use to clean and aromatize the kitchen.
