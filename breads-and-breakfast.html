20. 2-Ingredient Biscuits

Image: Fluffy, golden brown homemade biscuits, fresh from the oven, perhaps with butter

Ingredients
  • 2 cups Self-rising Flour
  • 1 ½ cups Heavy Whipping Cream
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Mix flour and cream well. Form a ball.
  3. Do not knead. Gently fold the dough over itself.
  4. Roll or pat out and cut biscuits. You can use a floured bottle top.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.

21. Raisin Buns (12 Buns)

Image: A batch of fluffy homemade raisin buns

Ingredients
  • 1 C. Milk
  • 1 Tbsp. Instant Yeast
  • 6 Tbsp. Melted Butter/Margarine
  • 1/2 C. Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1 1/2 C. Raisins
  • 1/4 tsp. Lemon Zest
  • 1/4 tsp. Salt
  • 4 C. Flour
  • Cinnamon & Nutmeg (to taste)
Method
  1. Warm milk, add yeast, set aside until risen.
  2. Beat eggs with lemon zest, add milk mixture.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  4. Knead dough for 5-7 mins.
  5. Let rise for 2 hours or until double in size.
  6. Form rolls, place in baking pan, and let proof for another hour.
  7. Bake. (Temp/time not specified)

Notes: Bake commonly at 350°F for ~20–25 min until golden.

22. Cookist (Savory Fritters)

Image: Savory fritters with visible bits of ham and carrot

Ingredients
  • 200 ml (0.8 cup) Water
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp. Sugar
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 10 gr Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp. Paprika
  • 200 gr (1.6 cup) All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Carrot
  • 50 gr Ham
  • Green Onions
  • Oil (for frying)
Method
  1. Mix water, eggs, sugar, and yeast. Add flour and stir.
  2. Let rise 20 mins.
  3. Add salt, paprika, sausage (ham), carrots, and green onions.
  4. Fry with a ladle (like fritters or pancakes).

24. Pan Francés (French Bread)

Image: A crusty artisan loaf of French bread

Ingredients
  • 800 gr Wheat Flour
  • 640 gr Water
  • 20 gr Salt
  • 7 gr Dry Yeast
Method
  1. Combine flour, yeast, and salt. Add water and mix.
  2. Cover dough and let rest for 1 hour.
  3. Pre-shape the dough, then fold (3 times).
  4. Place dough in a preheated Dutch Oven. Cover.
  5. Bake at 250°C (480°F) for 30 min.
  6. Remove lid, continue baking for 20 min until brown.
  7. Let cool for 1 hour before cutting.

25. Make your own Pizza! (Quick)

Image: A homemade pepperoni pizza, fresh from the oven

Base Ingredients
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 C Oil
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 tsp. Baking Powder
  • 2 C Flour
Toppings
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Grated Mozzarella
  • Tomato Slices
  • Oregano
  • Pepperoni
Method
  1. Blend eggs, oil, salt, and baking powder.
  2. Add flour and blend again.
  3. Place on a pizza pan with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 15 min (350°F implied).
  5. Take out, add all toppings.
  6. Bake another 15 min at 350°F.

26. Bread in a bag

Image: A loaf of homemade bread, representing the 'bread in a bag' recipe

Ingredients
  • 4 cups Flour (plus 2 cups extra)
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 pkg. Yeast
  • 1 cup Warm Water (plus 1 cup extra)
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Melted Butter
  • 1 tsp. Salt
  • 1 Egg
Method
  1. In a Ziploc bag, mix sugar, yeast, 1 cup warm water, and milk. Seal and press air out.
  2. Let sit on counter for 10 min until bubbly.
  3. Add salt, 1 cup flour, butter, and egg. Shake and mix.
  4. Add remaining flour. Mix with hands until a solid dough forms.
  5. Knead on a floured surface for 10 min.
  6. Place in a greased pan, cover, let sit for 30-45 min.
  7. Bake 30 mins. (Temp not specified)

Notes: Typical bake 350°F for ~30 min until golden.

27. Cinnamon Rolls

Image: A pan of gooey, delicious cinnamon rolls with white cream cheese icing drizzled on top

Dough
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1 pkg. Yeast
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 4 oz Butter
Filling Paste
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • 3 oz Butter
  • 1 tsp. Flour
Icing
  • ½ cup Castor Sugar
  • ½ cup Philadelphia Cream Cheese
  • 2 tsp. Milk
Method
  1. Mix dough ingredients and let rest for 1 hour.
  2. Roll dough flat. Mix filling ingredients to form a paste and spread all over.
  3. Roll up, cut, and place in a greased pan. Put butter over the top.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes.
  5. Mix icing ingredients and spread all over the top after baking.

28. Orange Buns

Image: Sweet, golden orange buns with a sticky glaze

Ingredients
  • 1 1/2 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 1 Tbsp. Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/2 tsp. Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Tbsp. Condensed Milk
  • 1/2 tsp. Cinnamon
  • Orange Zest
Syrup
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • Orange Juice (to flavor)
Method
  1. Sift dry ingredients. Cut in butter.
  2. Add egg, milk, vanilla, condensed milk, cinnamon, and zest.
  3. Knead dough softly. Pat into 12 pieces.
  4. Let rise to double in size.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 30 min until golden.
  6. Boil sugar and water for syrup; remove from heat, add orange juice.
  7. Brush buns with syrup.

29. Hops Bread

Image: Fluffy, golden-brown hops bread rolls

Ingredients
  • 4 1/2 C. All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 2 tsp. Sugar
  • 2 1/2 C. Warm Milk
  • 4 Tbsp. Melted Margarine/Butter
  • 2 1/2 tsp. Yeast
Method
  1. Mix milk, yeast, etc. (rest of add flour & bread).
  2. Let rest for 1 hour.
  3. Knead again and rest 45 mins.
  4. Form into balls & let rise.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 20 mins.
  6. Brush with butter or margarine.
